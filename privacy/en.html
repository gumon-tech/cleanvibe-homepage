Privacy Policy for Customer

ASIA CONNECT CORPORATION COMPANY LIMITED recognizes the importance of protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, or disclosure of personal data, including other rights of the Data Subjects in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Laws.

Collection of Personal Data

We will collect your personal data that we receive directly from you as follows:

  • Account registration
  • Telephone number
  • Email address
  • Facebook Login
  • Google Login
  • Face vector data for time attendance (facial recognition for clock-in/out purposes)

Types of Data Collected

  • Personal data: Name, surname, age, date of birth, nationality, identification card, passport, etc.
  • Contact information: Address, telephone number, email address, etc.
  • Account details: Username, password, transaction history, etc.
  • Proof of identity: Copy of identification card, passport, etc.
  • Transaction and Financial information: Purchase history, credit card details, bank account information, etc.
  • Technical data: IP address, Cookie ID, Activity Log, etc.
  • Face data: Face vector data used for clocking in and out.
  • Other: Photo, video, and other information that is considered personal data under the Personal Data Protection Laws.

Children

If you are under the age of 20 or have legal restrictions, we may collect, use, or disclose your personal data. We require parental or guardian consent or as allowed by applicable laws. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from children without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Storage of Data

We store your personal data in both hard copy and soft copy formats using the following systems:

  • Our servers in Thailand
  • Our servers outside of Thailand
  • Third-party server service providers in Thailand
  • Third-party server service providers outside of Thailand

Face data for time attendance is stored locally on the device used for clock-in/out and is encrypted.

Use of Data

We use the collected data for various purposes:

  • To create and manage accounts
  • To provide products or services
  • To improve products, services, or user experiences
  • To share and manage information within our organization
  • To conduct marketing activities and promotions
  • To provide after-sales services
  • To gather user feedback
  • To process payments for products or services
  • To verify employee attendance using facial recognition for clock-in/out
  • To comply with our Terms and Conditions
  • To comply with laws, rules, and regulatory authorities

Disclosure of Personal Data

We may disclose your personal data to the following parties in certain circumstances:

  • Organization: Within our organization to develop our products and services.
  • Service Providers: We may use service providers to help us offer our services, such as payments, marketing, and product development. Please note that these service providers have their own privacy policies.
  • Business Partners: We may share certain personal data with business partners to coordinate services.
  • Law Enforcement: Under certain circumstances, we may be required to disclose your personal data if required by law or in response to requests by government authorities, such as courts or regulatory bodies.

Face Data

What face data does the app collect?
The app collects face vector data to identify employees for clock-in/out.

Planned uses of face data
The face data is used exclusively for authenticating employees when clocking in or out to ensure accurate timekeeping.

Will the face data be shared with any third parties?
No, the face data is not shared with third parties and is stored locally on the device.

How long will face data be retained?
Face data will be retained only during the employment period and deleted once the employment ends or when no longer necessary.

Where is this explained in the policy?

  • Disclosure of Personal Data

Cross-Border Data Transfer

We may transfer personal data, including face data, to third parties, organizations, or servers located in foreign countries. We take steps to ensure that personal data is securely transferred with the necessary protection measures, as permitted by law.

Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary during the period you are a customer or in a relationship with us, or for as long as necessary in connection with the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will erase, destroy, or anonymize your personal data when it is no longer needed or the period lapses. Face data will be deleted once the employment ends or is no longer necessary.

Data Subject Rights

You have the following rights under the Personal Data Protection Laws:

  • Withdrawal of consent: You may withdraw your consent for the collection, use, or disclosure of your personal data.
  • Data access: You may request access to your personal data, copies of it, and how we obtained it.
  • Data portability: You may request the transfer of your personal data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: You may object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Data erasure or destruction: You may request the erasure, destruction, or anonymization of your personal data if it is no longer necessary.
  • Suspension: You may request the suspension of processing while rectification or objection requests are being examined.
  • Rectification: You may request the correction of your personal data.
  • Complaint lodging: You have the right to file a complaint if you believe your data rights have been violated.

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance user experience, track usage, and store preferences. If you do not wish to allow cookies, you may adjust your browser settings.

Data Security

We protect your personal data with security measures based on confidentiality, integrity, and availability principles. Face data is encrypted and stored locally on the device.

Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify the relevant authorities within 72 hours and inform you of any risks or remedial measures, depending on the severity.
